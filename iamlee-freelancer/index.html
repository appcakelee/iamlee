All about me

Hi there, I am Lee...
Front End Web Developer from London now based in Singapore.

With over 10 years experience in design and development I have spent the best part of the last 8 years in the fashion industry leading a small team of talented developers creating the many incarnations of jackwills.com.

Feeling a need for a change we (my wife and I) relocated to Singapore in search of new challenges and opportunities.

As a front end developer my technical skills include, but not limited to: HTML5, CSS3 (& LESS + Sass), Responsive Web Design (RWD), JADE, jQuery, Umbraco (Open Source CMS), Bootstrap, XSLT, Wordpress. Alongside my main skills I have had exposure to ASP.NET MVC, Javascript, PHP. I am also fully versed in the Adobe CS suite including video editing and encoding.

Currently I am looking for permanent work here in Singapore so if you have a role you think I would suit please download my most up to date CV/Resume and get in contact
